Services

Wildland Gardens is more than a wholesale and retail nursery. While we supply native and drought-adapted plants and offer state-of-the-art landscaping and design, we also provide a unique glamping experience with packages that include ATV/bike rental and excursion opportunities at Big Rock Candy Mountain and easy access to state and national parks. Additionally, our indoor and outdoor facilities can accommodate your special event!

Landscaping

Yard Design

You'll get water-wise trees, shrubs and wildflowers from Wildland Gardens for your xeriscaped yard that creates a beautiful, low maintenance property that preserves our natural resources.

Native Plants

Trees - Shrubs - Wildflowers

Get Utah's annual flowers, perennial plants, rose bushes and shrubs from Wildland Gardens. We also offer a free on-site estimate to Sevier Valley residents.

Glamping

Garden Guesthouse - Small Cabin - Tents

Guests will have access to a bathroom, water, the property and events as well as a private picnic area. Guesthouse comfortably houses 12 guests.

Rentals

ATVs - Bikes - Rafting

There's never a dull moment at Wildland Gardens. We can arrange group events or intimate excursions from trail riding to ziplining. We have your outdoor adventure covered. You can rent a mountain bike or ATV to explore the Sevier Valley or even go river rafting.

Parks

State & National Parks

You have easy access to nearby Big Rock Candy Mountain and the Fremont Indian State Park. Wildland Gardens is ideal for an overnight getaway or perfect for your family reunion vacation with additional access to Zion National Park and Bryce Canyon National Park.

Events

Farm Fest - Classes - More
