About Us

Wildland Gardens was founded in 2018 in Joseph, Utah, on hundreds of acres of farmlands that have been transformed into a glamping oasis with beautifully landscaped grounds, cozy cabin and high-end tents.

Wildland Gardens is the vision come true from the hard work and dedication of Mike and Carrie Ann Richards.

The couple has created a co-op farm and gardening learning space practicing sustainability through native plant, farm to table, and prioritizing local products and services. Their property is evolving to use renewable power and recyclable resources, and sustain a year-round garden learning environment for their guests.

  • Experience one-of-kind, cozy cabin space, which includes electric heat, lights and fantastic views of the Sevier Valley and gardens.
  • Enjoy a solitary time or romantic getaway in beautiful, rural Utah with breathtaking mountain landscapes.
  • Common area includes hot water, shower area and complimentary coffee/tea/hot chocolate.

Take the opportunity to experience the fresh air and goodness of rural Utah living. You won't regret it! You don't have to take our word for it. Our guests say it all.
