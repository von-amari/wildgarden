Stay At The Gardens

This isolated property has six different listings. Our space is evolving to use renewable power and recyclable resources, and sustain a year-round garden learning environment for our guests. There are beautiful views of the gardens and mountain landscapes. A perfect stop while traveling cross-country or visiting Utah's Big 5 National Parks. Hiking, mountain biking, river rafting, ATV trails and Mystic Hot Springs are nearby. We are dog friendly.

Glamping Tent

This high quality glamping tent is located in a beautiful landscape with incredible views of the scenic Sevier Valley. Tent includes a comfy Queen size bed, a sofa/futon sitting area, access to the community fire pit, a shared shower and restroom. Hiking, biking, horseback riding, ATV trails and National Parks are all nearby.

Garden Guesthouse

*Now includes Air Conditioning! This one of kind cozy cabin space includes an electric heat, lights and fantastic views of the Sevier Valley and gardens. Enjoy a solitary time or romantic getaway in this one of a kind beautiful space. Bathroom facilities and a common area are available in the main house which is about a 30 yard walk.

The Inn

Our unique home has 2 bedrooms and a common area sleeping space. We have a total of 1 King, 4 Queen , and 2 twin sized beds. It is convenient, relaxing & beautiful.

Group Camping

Book all 3 tent to for a unique camp experience for your group of 6-12. Each tent can accommodate up to 4 campers with a queen bed and full-size futon. All tents are within 20 yards of each other. Tent 2 and 3 share a common space and are 3 yards apart.
